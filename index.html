Who are we?

We are two friends who founded Snowberry Games in January 2025 after starting to work on our first commercial title, The Firelight Inspired by Larian Studios and Hazelight, we aim to create co-op games that bring people closer together.

Mano Marichal is lead designer and programmer. He studied Computer Science at Antwerp and Game Studies & Engineering in Austria, interned at Dirty Paws Studio and co-founded Snowberry Games.

Florian Heynen is lead artist, narrative designer, and sound designer. With a lifelong passion for art and games, he studied Interactive Sound Production at DAE and worked at Demute and co-founded Snowberry Games.
